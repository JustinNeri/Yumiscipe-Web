List of Dishes

Foods Description Rating

Adobo

 Adobo is a beloved Filipino dish known for its savory, tangy, and slightly sweet flavors. It typically consists of meat (such as chicken, pork, or beef) marinated in a mixture of soy sauce, vinegar, garlic, and spices, then simmered until tender. The result is a rich and flavorful dish that pairs perfectly with steamed rice.

Bulalo

 Bulalo is a traditional Filipino beef soup known for its rich and hearty flavors. It features tender beef shanks with bone marrow, simmered with vegetables like corn, cabbage, and potatoes in a flavorful broth. The marrow adds a luxurious texture to the soup, making it a comforting and satisfying dish, especially on a cold day.

Bicol Express

 Bicol Express is a spicy Filipino dish from the Bicol region. It consists of pork cooked in coconut milk and chili peppers, creating a creamy and fiery flavor profile. The dish is often seasoned with shrimp paste and garlic, adding depth to its taste. Bicol Express is a favorite among those who enjoy spicy and flavorful cuisine.

Sinigang

 Sinigang is a traditional Filipino sour soup known for its tangy and savory flavors. It is typically made with pork, shrimp, or fish, along with a variety of vegetables such as tomatoes, radishes, and kangkong (water spinach). The soup is flavored with tamarind, giving it a distinct sour taste that is both refreshing and comforting.

Caldereta

 Caldereta is a rich and hearty Filipino stew made with meat (usually beef or goat), potatoes, carrots, and bell peppers. The dish is cooked in a tomato-based sauce with liver spread, giving it a unique and savory flavor. Caldereta is often enjoyed during special occasions and family gatherings, served with steamed rice.

Kare-Kare

 Kare-Kare is a traditional Filipino stew known for its rich and creamy peanut sauce. It is typically made with oxtail, tripe, and vegetables such as eggplant, string beans, and banana blossoms. The dish is often served with bagoong (fermented shrimp paste) on the side, adding a salty and savory contrast to the sweet and nutty flavors of the stew.

Hamburger

 A hamburger is a popular American sandwich featuring a cooked patty of ground meat (usually beef) placed inside a sliced bun. It is often garnished with lettuce, tomato, onions, pickles, cheese, and condiments such as ketchup and mustard. Hamburgers are a staple of fast food and casual dining, enjoyed by people of all ages.

Mac n Cheese

 Mac and cheese is a classic comfort food dish with roots in American cuisine. It consists of cooked macaroni pasta mixed with a creamy cheese sauce, often made with cheddar cheese. The dish is baked until bubbly and golden, creating a rich and satisfying meal that is loved by both children and adults.

Garlic Fried Chicken

 Garlic fried chicken is a flavorful and crispy dish where chicken pieces are marinated in a mixture of garlic, soy sauce, and spices, then deep-fried until golden and crunchy. The garlic adds a robust and aromatic flavor to the chicken, making it a popular choice for those who enjoy bold and savory dishes.

BBQ Ribs

 BBQ ribs are a delicious and popular dish featuring pork or beef ribs that are seasoned with a dry rub or marinated in a flavorful sauce, then slow-cooked or grilled to perfection. The result is tender, juicy meat with a smoky and savory taste, often enjoyed with a side of coleslaw or baked beans.
