ABOUT YUMISCIPE

Founder of Yumiscipe

Yumicipe comes from 'Yumi's Recipe,' which is called after the creator Yumi, who was his favorite name. Yumicipe is a dynamic website created to share a wide variety of recipes, from easy go-to dinners to complex feasts with direct to the point manner. Yumicipe features recipes from a variety of cultures and dietary needs, so it has something to offer everyone, whether they are home cooks or lovers of food. By encouraging innovation in the kitchen, the website hopes to make cooking approachable and pleasurable for people of different culinary abilities. The website is created since the founder is passionate about cooking
